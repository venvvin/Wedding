Things to Do

We chose Rimini not only for our wedding, but also for its atmosphere. Here are a few places and experiences we think you’ll love.

Historic Center

Walk through the old town, visit Arco di Augusto and Ponte di Tiberio.

Beach & Sunset

Enjoy long sandy beaches and beautiful sunsets by the sea.

Food & Aperitivo

Try local pasta, seafood and don’t miss an evening aperitivo.

Gelato Stops

Take a break with authentic Italian gelato around the city.

San Marino Trip

A short trip to San Marino offers stunning views and a unique atmosphere.

Just Relax

Slow down, enjoy the vibe, coffee, and time with friends and family.
